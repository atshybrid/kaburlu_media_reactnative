## ğŸ¨ Design Specifications

### **Card Sizes**
- **Selected Card:** Full width, 200px height
- **Grid Cards:** 48% width, 160px height
- **Border radius:** 16px (more modern)
- **Elevation:** 4 (more prominent shadow)

### **Typography**
- **Native Name:** 32px, Bold, Language-specific font
- **English Name:** 18px, Medium, System font
- **Region:** 14px, Regular, Gray (#666)
- **Stats:** 12px, Regular, Light gray (#999)

### **Color System**
- Each card has gradient background (not just bottom border)
- Text color adapts based on background (white/black for contrast)
- Checkmark: White circle with green âœ“
- Border: Subtle white outline on selected

### **Animations**
- **Card press:** Scale down to 0.95
- **Selection:** Rotate checkmark in + subtle bounce
- **Scroll reveal:** Fade in from bottom (stagger animation)

### **Icon Style**
- **Format:** Custom SVG icons
- **Size:** 80x80px (larger, more prominent)
- **Position:** Center top of card
- **Style:** Minimalist line art with cultural accuracy
